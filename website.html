Our Story

Nature-Inspired Radiance

Sunkissed was born from a love of warm summer days and healthy, glowing skin. Founded in 2018 by skincare enthusiast Maya Rodriguez, our mission is to create products that enhance your natural beauty without harsh chemicals.

All our products are vegan, cruelty-free, and formulated with at least 90% naturally derived ingredients. We believe in sustainable beauty that's good for your skin and the planet.

Every Sunkissed product is designed to give you that fresh-from-vacation glow, no matter the season or your skin tone.
